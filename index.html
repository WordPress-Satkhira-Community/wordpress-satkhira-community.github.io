About Us

The WordPress Satkhira Community: Fostering Knowledge Sharing and Collaboration in Bangladesh.

In the bustling city of Satkhira, nestled in the southwestern corner of Bangladesh, a vibrant and passionate community of web enthusiasts and developers has been steadily growing. This close-knit group, known as the WordPress Satkhira Community, shares a common bond – their unwavering passion for WordPress. Together, they have created a thriving ecosystem that promotes knowledge sharing, collaboration, and the love for all things WordPress.

Our Goals

The WordPress Satkhira Community is driven by a clear set of goals. We aim to organize local meetups to facilitate discussions and connections among WordPress enthusiasts. Additionally, we aspire to host WordCamps, providing a platform for in-depth WordPress-focused events.

Our commitment extends to building valuable WordPress plugins and themes, offering workshops for hands-on learning experiences, and maintaining an active online community for discussions and knowledge sharing. Inclusivity is at our core, ensuring that individuals of all skill levels and backgrounds are welcome. We also aim to grow our community’s reach, collaborate with local tech organizations, and ultimately empower our members with valuable digital skills for personal and professional success. Together, we strive to strengthen the WordPress community in Satkhira, Bangladesh, and contribute to the growth of the tech ecosystem.

Our Events

At the WordPress Satkhira Community, our events are all about fostering a spirit of collaboration, continuous learning, and community building.

We encourage you to join us in any or all of our events, whether you’re a seasoned WordPress expert or just taking your first steps into the world of WordPress. Together, we’re committed to supporting one another, expanding our WordPress knowledge, and making a positive impact in the Satkhira tech community. Stay tuned for updates on upcoming events and let’s explore the limitless possibilities of WordPress together!
